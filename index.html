About

I am a Front End Web Developer and Designer, and an award winning Filmmaker. I design and develop responsive websites using HTML and CSS, and I have some knowledge of Javascript implementation.

Currently I have just completed the Web Development Bootcamp at Yoobee School of Design where I learned coding, design, responsive development, UX, UI, and frameworks. I am now freelancing and have recently completed work for Double Happy Design and Veratech.

I have a Bachelors in English Literature from Victoria University of Wellington and a Diploma in Filmmaking from SAE Institute, Auckland, and my short films have been featured in Film festivals worldwide.

Salvation Army landing page

This template was made for a class assignment for a landing page for the Salvation army. We were put into pairs where I was the designer, then me and my partner worked together to peer code the site.

The goal of this landing page was to increase their Facebook likes, so I designed something that made the user feel a certain emotion - empathy. I chose a hero image that created this emotion, plus a title that would make people want to push the CTA. I made sure that the clients colors and branding were adhered to, and that the page was clean, clear and consise.

Yoobee Mobile

This is a mobile first redesign for the Yoobee site, made for an assignment at Yoobee. The brief specified that there must be a CTA, so I wrote a headline that would entice the user to press the 'click here to learn more' button. I also created a 'choose your language' button to help international students.

Once this was done I built it using Bootstrap, then used Phonegap to turn it into an app. I used an Iframe to resize it so as to display how it would look on a mobile phone.

El Mensaje

Juanita sits waiting in a cafe for Ricardo. When he arrives they begin a conversation without saying a word. As the conversation unfolds it becomes clear that Juanita is there for more than just coffee.

Written and directed by me, El Mensaje is a black comedy satire with a devilish twist. It is a reflection on the trends of modern society and pays homage to the old saying, 'Hell hath no fury like a woman scorned'.
